About the Exits column. The raw export reports the last item a child saw. Because a child who left after seeing item X actually exited the app when the next item appeared, every value has been shifted down by one position along the actual administration order (p01 → p02 → q01 → q02 → … → q17). We therefore cannot know how many children exited at p01 (the first item), and the 212 children whose last item was q17 are counted as having completed the assessment rather than as exits. Listening animations are excluded from this table because they do not appear in the exit-rates export. Practice items do not count toward the assessment score.
